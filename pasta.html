Pasta salad recipe

Desciption of the recipe

This delicious Awesome Pasta Salad is a colorful and refreshing dish that's perfect for picnics, potlucks, or summer meals. It begins with cooked tri-color rotini pasta, which is tossed with a vibrant mix of chopped vegetables like bell peppers, cucumbers, and tomatoes. The salad is generously coated in Italian-style salad dressing, giving it a tangy and savory flavor. Optional add-ins like sliced olives, cubed cheese, or pepperoni can elevate the dish even further. Chilled before serving, this pasta salad offers a delightful blend of textures and tastes in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package tri-color rotini pasta
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the tri-color rotini pasta and cook according to package directions until al dente.
  3. Drain the pasta and rinse under cold water to cool it down.
  4. Transfer the cooled pasta to a large mixing bowl.
  5. Add cherry tomatoes, chopped cucumber, green and red bell peppers, and black olives to the bowl.
  6. Pour in the Italian-style salad dressing and toss everything together until well coated.
  7. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Refrigerate the salad for at least one hour before serving to enhance the flavors.
