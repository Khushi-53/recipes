Chicken makhani recipe

Description of the recipe

Chicken Makhani, also known as Butter Chicken, is a popular Indian dish featuring tender chicken simmered in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. Flavored with warm spices like garam masala and turmeric, it’s both comforting and flavorful. Often served with rice or naan, it's a favorite for its smooth, mildly spiced taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste (optional)
  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil (for cooking chicken)

Preparation steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and sauté until soft and translucent.
  3. Stir in the garlic and ginger, and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add garam masala, chili powder, and turmeric; cook for 30 seconds while stirring to toast the spices.
  5. Pour in the tomato sauce and simmer for 10–15 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream, yogurt, and butter. Simmer on low heat until the sauce thickens slightly. Season with salt and cayenne pepper to taste.
  7. In a separate skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium heat.
  8. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned and no longer pink in the center.
